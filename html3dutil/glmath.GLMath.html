(static) vec3cross(a, b) → {Array.<number>}

Finds the cross product of two 3-element vectors. The cross product is the vector that is perpendicular to each of two other vectors. If both vectors are unit length (via glmath.GLMath.vec3norm), the sine of the angle between them is equal to the length of their cross product.

The cross product (c) of vectors a and b is found as follows:

c.x = a.y * b.z - a.z * b.y
c.y = a.z * b.x - a.x * b.z
c.z = a.x * b.y - a.y * b.x
